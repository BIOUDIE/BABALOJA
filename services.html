Where We Shop

Ibadan's Best Markets

We know exactly which market to visit for each type of item — so you get the freshest quality at the best price.

Bodija Market
Best for: Grains, Tubers, Beans, Rice, Dry goods

The largest food market in Ibadan. Babaloja has established relationships with key wholesalers for consistently competitive grain and tuber prices.

Oja'ba Market
Best for: Fresh Perishables, Peppers, Tomatoes, Vegetables

Ibadan's premier fresh produce hub. We know the vendors who source directly from farms — meaning fresher produce that lasts longer.

Akesan Market
Best for: Proteins, Oils, Spices, Mixed items

A well-stocked market with excellent protein traders and spice merchants who provide reliable quality and fair pricing.

Gbagi Market
Best for: Wholesale goods, Bulk orders

When you need large quantities at the most competitive prices, Gbagi is where Babaloja goes for wholesale sourcing.
