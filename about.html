Who We Are

Ibadan's Personal Market Insider

Babaloja — meaning Market Father or Head of the Market in Yoruba — is a professional grocery procurement and delivery service built specifically for Ibadan.

We exist because navigating the massive crowds and heat of Bodija, Gbagi, or Oja'ba markets consumes your most valuable asset: time. Whether you're a working professional, a busy household, a restaurant owner, or someone who simply values their Saturdays — Babaloja is your premium personal eyes and hands on the market floor.

We specialise exclusively in fresh foodstuffs. By focusing on one thing and doing it exceptionally well, we've built relationships with the best vendors in every major Ibadan market. We know which seller has the crispest peppers, which stall offers the best price-per-bucket of garri, and how to verify high-quality cuts of meat on your behalf.

Every kobo you spend with Babaloja is fully accounted for. Our itemised receipt is shared after every run — no mystery charges, no inflated prices, no surprises at the door.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Trust Above All

Trust is everything in this business. We earn it through radical transparency — live market updates, itemised receipts, and a zero-surprise policy on pricing.

02

Specialisation

We only do foodstuffs. Not electronics, not fashion — food. This focus means we know our markets, our vendors, and the seasonal pricing cycles better than anyone.

03

Freshness First

Every item is selected with the same care we'd apply to our own household shopping. Perishables are bought last, packed on top, and delivered fast.

04

Fair Pricing

Our 5% service fee is flat and transparent. We don't inflate prices to make a hidden margin. What the market charges is what you pay — plus just 5%.
