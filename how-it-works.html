How Babaloja Works

From your WhatsApp message to fresh groceries at your gate — here's every step of the process.

01
📋

Send Your Shopping List

Open WhatsApp and send us your full shopping list. Be as specific as possible — include quantities using Ibadan market measurements like paint buckets, crates, kilograms, or naira amounts.

Example message to send:
Hi Babaloja! I need:
- Rice: 10kg
- Fresh tomatoes: 1 crate
- Ata rodo (scotch bonnet): 1 paint bucket
- Red onions: ₦2,000 worth (small ones)
- Palm oil: 4 litres
- Beef: 1kg (cut into chunks)
Delivery to: Jericho GRA, Ibadan
Start Here on WhatsApp

After shopping, we share our own full itemised receipt showing exactly what was purchased and at what price. Zero hidden charges — every kobo accounted for.

🚚

Fresh Delivery to Your Door

Your groceries are carefully packed — perishables always on top to prevent bruising — and delivered fresh to your address in Ibadan.

Packing guarantee: Soft perishables like tomatoes, peppers and leafy greens are always packed last and placed on top of heavier items. Your produce arrives market-fresh.
Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a market run take?

Most runs are completed and delivered same-day. We recommend sending your list the night before or early morning to guarantee same-day delivery.

What if the market prices change?

All estimates are based on live market rates. If the final price is lower, we refund you immediately. If higher, we contact you for approval before buying.

What areas in Ibadan do you deliver to?

We deliver across Ibadan — GRA, Jericho, Bodija residential, New Bodija, Ring Road, Agodi, Oluyole, Mokola and surrounding areas. Contact us if you're unsure about your area.

Can I see the market receipts?

We share our own full itemised Babaloja receipt detailing every item purchased and its price. This gives you complete transparency over your spend.

Do you handle bulk or restaurant orders?

Yes — we handle bulk orders for homes, restaurants, caterers and businesses. WhatsApp us your requirements and we'll work out the best approach.

How do subscriptions work?

Weekly or monthly subscription customers get priority slots and preferential service fees. Contact us on WhatsApp to set up a recurring order.
